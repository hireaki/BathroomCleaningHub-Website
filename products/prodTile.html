TILE & GROUT CLEANER

Grout / Tiles cleaner chemicals are a bit acidic, it is a specialized water-based solutions that ensure deep cleaning of multiple surfaces.These remove dirt, water stains, and old & stubborn residues to restore the original shine and appearance of the tiles.


PROPER USAGE & DISPOSAL

For a minor spot, start with mixing a tile cleaner with water for the product to take better effect. Use a clean cloth to dip in the solution and rub the tile gently to remove spots. Dilute two full caps of the tile cleaner in half a bucket of water. It preserves the natural color of the tiles and prevents any grease, oil, or dust build-up. Grout / Tile Cleaner products can usually be disposed of by pouring it down the drain slowly with running water. Never pour metal polishes, wood polishes, waxes, and other solvent-based cleaners down the drain. Instead, keep them until you can dispose of them with the hazardous waste collection day. Always check the label for directions of proper disposal.


PRECAUTIONARY MEASURES

Keep the work area clean and organized to minimize trip hazards and accidents. Lift and handle heavy tiles carefully to avoid strain or injury. Use proper lifting techniques and ask for help if needed. When handling grouts, or cleaning agents, avoid direct contact with the skin and follow the mixing instructions carefully. Use the recommended water-to-powder or resin-to-hardener ratios for best results.
