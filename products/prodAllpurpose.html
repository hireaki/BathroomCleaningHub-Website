ALL PURPOSE CLEANER

All-Purpose Cleaner a versatile product, eliminates viruses, bacteria, soap scum, and tough grease. It is available as liquids, in spray form or as wipes. It is formulated to effectively clean, disinfect, and remove dirt, and stains from multiple surfaces without separating different kinds of products.


PROPER USAGE & DISPOSAL

Fill a bucket with the appropriate amount of water and all purpose cleaner, then let the soap dissolve into the water. Use a mop to mix the soap and water before mopping. Mop your floors thoroughly with the cleaner, then squeeze the mop and go over the wet surfaces again to remove any excess water. If you are using a spray bottle, fill the bottle mostly full with warm water and the product, then, spray All-Purpose Home Cleaner on compatible surfaces. At last, wipe surfaces until clean and fully dry for a smooth finish. Read the label for disposal instructions. Some manufacturers provide guidelines for disposal. Use up all the product use the cleaner until it is empty. This is the most environmentally friendly option. Then, discard the container carefully with the trash or hazardous waste, if it contains harmful chemicals.


PRECAUTIONARY MEASURES

Keep out of reach of children. Don’t leave containers open. Wear protective clothing. Be sensitive to reactions that may occur after using a product. Use products in a well-ventilated area to avoid breathing any fumes. Wash hands after use.
