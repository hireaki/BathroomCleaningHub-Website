TOILET BOWL CLEANER

Toilet Bowl Cleaner is an effective stain remover that can remove dirt, rust, and urinals. It is available in different forms, including liquids, foams, and powders. By consistently disinfecting and cleaning your toilet, you can stop the spread of germs and bacteria, maintain a clean bathroom, and prevent buildup of stains.


PROPER USAGE & DISPOSAL

The first step in using the Toilet Bowl Cleaner is to pour the right amount of the product inside the toilet bowl surface, then leave the solution to react for a few minutes, after that be sure to brush the bowl well to remove the dirt or stains, lastly is to flush and rinse with water. The proper way to dispose Toilet bowl cleaner is to get rid of the product containers by sorting them out in the trash. If there is still product left inside, wash them down the drain. For stronger solutions, mix it in more water to lessen any chance to cause corrosion and to avoid from any possible reek of smell.


PRECAUTIONARY MEASURES

Keep out of reach of children. Avoid breathing mist or vapor by wearing a mask. Refrain contact with the eyes or the skin. Wear protective gloves. Wash hands thoroughly after use. Avoid using excessive amounts of toilet cleaner. Follow the recommended dosage provided by the manufacturer.
